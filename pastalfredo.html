Recipe

Ingredients

    1. Pasta of your choice
    2. Shrimp of you choice
    3. Olive oil
    4. Butter (could be unsalted or salted)
    5. Cajun seasoning
    6. Heavy cream
    7. Parmesan cheese
    8. Garlic Powder and Ground black pepper
    9. Green powder and chopped parsley

Pasta

    1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
    2. While the pasta cooks, season the shrimp with Cajun seasoning.
    3. Place butter and olive oil in a skillet and cook the shrimp for 1-2 minutes per side.
    4. Remove the shrimp from the skillet. Add the cream, the garlic powder and ground pepper. Simmer on low heat.
    5. At this point, your pasta should be about ready. Before draining it, reserve one cup of the pasta cooking water. (You will use the pasta water if the sauce gets too thick.)
    6. Add the Parmesan cheese to the sauce and simmer on low heat. The sauce should not boil. If it does, it will separate and look oily.
    7. Add the drained pasta into the skillet followed by the Cajun shrimp.
    8. Toss gently and season with additional Cajun seasoning to taste.
    9. Top with green onions and chopped parsley (optional).

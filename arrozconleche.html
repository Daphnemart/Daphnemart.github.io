Why I like this food

I love aroz con leche because it is sweet ans adding a little of cinnomone taste and rich texture.

Recipe

Ingredients

    1. 7 cups water
    2. 1 cup long-grain white rice
    3. 2 cinnamon sticks
    4. 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
    5. 1 (12-ounce) can evaporated milk
    6. 1 cup whole milk
    7. ground cinnamon for topping

Directions

    1. Combine water, rice, and cinnamon sticks in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
    2. Bring to a boil, uncovered, and cook for 16â€“18 minutes.
    3. Drain any remaining liquid, remove the cinnamon sticks, and return the rice to the pan over medium-high heat.
    4. Add sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and whole milk, and bring to a boil.
    5. Reduce the heat to low and cook for about 20 minutes, stirring constantly.
    6. The milk can easily burn, so it is important to stir constantly as it thickens.
    7. Divide rice among serving cups and sprinkle with cinnamon. Serve warm.
